Methodology & why it's built this way
  • Hard counts come straight from USCIS and the U.S. Department of Labor. They reflect official fiscal-year releases, not real-time feeds.
  • Remittance figures auto-update from the World Bank Indicators API (free, public, no key). Marked AUTO.
  • Estimates (unauthorized population, fiscal cost) come from named research orgs and are reviewed by a human before publishing. The fiscal-cost figure is a FAIR high-end estimate shown with the offsetting taxes-paid noted; Cato and CBO estimate lower. Showing the range is what keeps the site credible.
  • Deliberately excluded: off-the-books labor and "lost wages from Americans." Those can't be measured, only guessed — a fabricated counter would discredit every real number beside it.
  • Every figure links to its primary source so anyone can verify it.
Not affiliated with any government agency. Built for public transparency. Figures rounded for display; click any source for exact values.
